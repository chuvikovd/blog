Who I am

I am professional frontend developer from Latvia. I have strong background in developing small to large scale web applications, mostly using React. I also have good experience working with Redux, GraphQL, Typescript, not to mention HTML & CSS. At my everyday work I am on the road to fullstack developer with Ruby on Rails backend.
